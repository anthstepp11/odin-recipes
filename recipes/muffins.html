Blueberry Muffins

Description

A simple and quick blueberry muffin recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups blueberries

Steps

  1. pre heat oven to 350 and prepare 12 muffin cups with liners
  2. stir all ingredents together in large bowl
  3. spoon mixture equally into muffin cups
  4. bake 25 minutes
