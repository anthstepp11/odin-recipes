Classic Pancakes

Description

a simple quick pancake recipe that will not disappoint!

ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups pancake mix
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. heat skillet over medium heat.
  2. In medium bowl, stir together milk and eggs and add pancake mix.
  3. spoon 1/4 cup of mix into skillet, cook till bubbles appear, flip and cook till brown.
